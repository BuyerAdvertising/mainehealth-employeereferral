MaineHealth Employee Referral Program
Since January 2020, the MaineHealth Employee Referral Program has paid out $1,325,067 to over 1,200 employees.
We’re asking our MaineHealth community to refer individuals you believe will be strong additions to our team. Opportunities to earn are endless, as you can refer candidates for positions at any entity within our network of care.
If your referred candidate is hired, you can earn $1,000, $2,000 or $5,000 depending on the position and shift.
How to Refer

Encourage your friends, family and professional network to visit our career site, careersatmainehealth.org and apply online. Remember: Candidates MUST put the name of the referring employee on their application.

Below is a breakdown of bonus amounts you can receive. So, act now and refer your friends and family—your cash rewards await you!

Contact MaineHealth Talent with any questions: talentacquisition@mainehealth.org

Eligibility & Guidelines
  • $1,000 referral bonus for all jobs; prorated for part-time and per diem.
  • $2,000 referral bonus for “Featured” roles; prorated for part-time and per diem. Featured roles can be identified on the career site by using the “Featured” filter on the left-hand side when searching for jobs. Also indicated by a yellow star next to the job title.
  • $5,000 referral bonus for “Spotlight” roles; prorated for part-time and per diem. Spotlight roles can be identified on the career site by using the “Spotlight” filter on the left-hand side when searching for jobs. Also indicated by a red star next to the job title.

In order to qualify, the referred candidate must select Employee Referral as their source on the application and the employee's name must be entered in the free text box that appears once Employee Referral is selected.

All employees NOT at the manager level or above, or employed on the Talent team, are eligible for a referral bonus.

Referred candidates must be external to the MaineHealth system.

Employees can receive payment for referring candidates to any MaineHealth site, in addition to the site at which they are employed.

Payouts are made 1x after the referred employee has been with the system for 6 months. Both the referring employee and the referred hire MUST be employed at MaineHealth at the 6 month mark. Employee Referral Bonuses are taxable income.
